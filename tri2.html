Welcome to KUDMFCA

We are delighted to have you here! The Kingdom Unity Destiny Ministerial Fellowship of Churches Association (KUDMFCA) is a network committed to uniting churches and empowering clergy across regions. Here, you can learn more about our services, access valuable resources,and discover ways to join our ministry of reconciliation. Thank you for visiting, and we warmly invite you to be part of this dynamic fellowship!.

About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster fellowship, provide valuable resources, and support clergy welfare through a shared vision of faith and service.

Our Vision

To be a unified body that empowers clergy and church communities to fulfill their divine purpose through faith, fellowship, and service, impacting lives and advancing the ministry of reconciliation across nations..

Core Values

  • Unity in Faith – We believe in bringing together diverse ministries under a shared mission.
  • Integrity – We are committed to maintaining honesty and accountability in all we do.
  • Empowerment – We uplift and support clergy to achieve their full potential in ministry.
  • Service – We dedicate ourselves to serving the spiritual and social needs of our communities.
  • Compassion – We foster a spirit of love and care, mirroring Christ’s compassion for all.
